Program Lunar Jeep

The automatic pilot in our lunar jeep doesn't work! We need you to program the vehicle (marked blue) remotely and lead it to the base (marked green). Watch out for the rocks (marked grey). You have to plan the whole route before you launch the jeep.

  • U - up
  • D - down
  • L - left
  • R - right
  • Back - remove last command
  • Start - launch lunar jeep
